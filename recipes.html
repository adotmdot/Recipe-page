Baked Lemon Pepper Whole Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Chicken
  • 1 1/2 Tablespoons Lemon Pepper Seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon Adobo seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon Citrus Blend Seasoning
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Garlic powder
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Onion powder
  • 1/2 Tablespoon Parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon Thyme
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 teaspoon Black Pepper
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • 1 tablespoon Butter

Directions:

Clean and spatchcock the chicken. Pour the olive oil all over the chicken. Mix all seasoning spices in a bowl. Apply seasoning mixture all over the chicken (both sides). Pour melted butter over the chicken. Add a few lemon slices on top. Bake at 340°F for 1 hour, basting the chicken throughout.

Salmon w/ Spinach and Mushroom

Ingredients

  • 1 Salmon Fillet
  • 1 cup Fresh Spinach
  • 1/2 cup Sliced Mushrooms
  • 1 clove Garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Directions:

Heat olive oil in a pan. Add garlic and mushrooms, cook until soft. Add spinach and sauté until wilted. In a separate pan, cook salmon until golden on both sides. Serve salmon over the sautéed vegetables.

Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients

  • 2 Bell Peppers (cut in half, seeds removed)
  • 1/2 lb Ground Turkey
  • 1/4 Onion (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup Shredded Cheese
  • 1/2 cup Cauliflower Rice
  • Salt, Pepper, Garlic Powder to taste

Directions:

Preheat oven to 375°F. Cook turkey with onion and seasoning. Mix in cauliflower rice. Stuff the bell peppers and top with cheese. Bake for 20-25 minutes until peppers are soft and cheese is melted.

Air Fried Lemon Pepper Wings

Ingredients

  • 8 Chicken Wings
  • 1 tablespoon Lemon Pepper Seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1 tablespoon Olive Oil

Directions:

Preheat air fryer to 400°F. Toss wings in olive oil and seasoning. Air fry for 25 minutes or until crispy, flipping halfway. Serve hot.
